"The Pirates of Penzance" is a comic opera written by Gilbert and Sullivan. The "Modern Major General's Song" is a famous patter song from the operetta. In this song, Major-General Stanley lists his many accomplishments and skills, but humorously admits he knows little about military matters. The lyrics are known for their rapid-fire delivery and clever wordplay, showcasing the wit and humor typical of Gilbert and Sullivan's collaborations. The song has become iconic and is often performed in various adaptations and parodies. It's a delightful and whimsical piece that adds a touch of humor to the operetta's overall lighthearted and satirical tone.
